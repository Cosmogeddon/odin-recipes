Lemon Grass Delight

Vegan Lemon Grass

Tired of those days where there is nothing to eat? Satisfy your hunger with some Lemon Grass. We don't mean your standard lemongrass, you read that right 'Lemon Grass.' In just minutes you'll be filling up your vegan stomach and saving money. In addition, this recipe is quite climate friendly!


Recipe

  • Grass
    1. Gather some grass (be sure to avoid yellow patches if you have pets)
    2. Place it on your plate
  • Lemon
    1. Cut up a lemon
    2. Sprinkle lemon on grass
