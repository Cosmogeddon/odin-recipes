Vegan Lime Water

Have you had a hankering for an oxymoron? Well, wait not further! The benefits of disitlled water find themselves canceled out by the acidity of lime in the most current health fad. Worry not! Those who are worth impressing are the crystal-loving yogies who will be astounded by the restorative chi properties of this beverage.

Recipe

Yield: 2 servings. Time required: 5 minutes

  • Distilled Water
    1. Pour water into glass
  • Lime
    1. Cut lime
    2. Put lime into distilled water
  • Garnish
    1. Do not garnish
    2. Enjoy!
